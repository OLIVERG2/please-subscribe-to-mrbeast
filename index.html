A big problem and how we can help.

Guns. They are very bad things, and if they are in kids hands they are worse. There are a lot of things that take lives. But guns take them with ease. There must be a way to prevent them from getting into the wrong hands. One of those ways would be getting better security or even just making it harder to obtain a fire arm. Let's just take a quick look at how gun violence can be prevented.

Prevention For Kids

There are so many kids that die every year from gun violence and these are some good basic ways to prevent it. One good way is to teach kids simple ways to prevent or report gun violence. In the text To prevent gun violence, these peacemakers start with the basics, It says “Schools in 23 states have set up an anonymous tip line serving more than 5 million students in grades 6 through 12.” This is one good way for kids to prevent gun violence. Also thay said “What we found is that 10% of tips contain reference to a firearm," says Elyse Thulin, an assistant professor at the Institute for Firearm Injury Prevention at the University of Michigan and the lead author on the study.” This statistic shows that these programs are actually working. This means that if the students learn to use the site or hote line correctly they can possibly protect thousands of deaths just by using basic tools provided by the government. Further Steps for prevention

That may be many simple steps but that is not the only thing happening to prevent gun violence.
