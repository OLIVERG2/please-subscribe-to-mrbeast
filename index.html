What Is the Coral Triangle

And why it matters.
The coral triangle is a beautiful set of islands above australia. it is a breeding ground for fish where most of the fish from around the world go to reproduce.
The Coral Triangle is also home to almost 76% of the worlds coral. As the tourist population grows, it is getting out of hand.

Natural Resources

Consumption.
Average consumption is 24-42 gallons of water a day. Land is being cleared for hotels.
Overfishing is becoming a major threat as demand for seafood grows.
