What Is the Coral Triangle

And why it matters.
The coral triangle is a beautiful set of islands above australia. the coral triangle is a breeding ground for fish where most of the fish from around the world go to reproduce. the coral triangle is aslo a big tourist destination witch has grown the economy in that area. as the tourist population grows so does the local population and it is getting out of hand.
The Coral Triangle is also home to almost 76% of the worlds coral.
